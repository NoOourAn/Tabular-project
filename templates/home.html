Tabular overview.

Tabular is a simple website that help you to generate an exam timetable easily in a short time with low cost.
If you are an organization and get the access code which you will give to your students to access their exams timetable .


As an organization. You can:

Create your account on Tabular with low cost to enjoy our services which requires you to upload specified data in a certain format , so you can create the exam timetables you want.


As a Student. All you have to do is:

Create your account on Tabular for free or log-in if you already have one ,then use the access code that was given to you by your organization to search for your exams timetable.
